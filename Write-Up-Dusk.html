Random Student Thoughts

Dusk

"Why is it better to talk at night?"

You asked.

I couldn't really respond but I was thinking.

Thinking...

And thinking...

Until the words escaped my mouth.

"Maybe because in the daylight..."

"One can clearly see what is in front of them..."

"At night you are given an endless void of darkness to fill."

"A darkness to fill with something you dream of but don't have."

You laughed and answered "Maybe."

That thought has never left my mind ever since.

Until now that I only see you at night.
