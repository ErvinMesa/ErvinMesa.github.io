Random Student Thoughts

Purpose

Denied of all opportunity the walls are thick.

Your efforts, your actions, voided.

Words punch down your throat following a kick.

My fate has been decided.

So don't ponder the death of a son.

The weight of the decline was unbearable.

It may feel like an overreaction.

Along with his dreams, he was buried, hopeful.

Say goodbye to my past self.

He's gone, he remained in the past.

He dissolved, his ambitions got old.

Its unresolved, they say he was cold.

At least that's what's been told.

His soul must have been sold.

So I cut off the wings I've grown.

I forget about my hopes, they're due.

I recall the future I've never known.

The truth, dreams don't come true.
