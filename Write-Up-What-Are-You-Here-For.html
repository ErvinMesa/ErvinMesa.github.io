Random Student Thoughts

What are you here for?

The creation of stories?

Bring curiosity through inquiries?

Bearing thorough enlightenment.

Whilst instilling a dark environment.

Fearing yet not appalling, scary yet interesting.

Back to the inquiry, what to ask?

The search for the answer truly a heavy task.

The reason you fight, your cause, what is it?

Are you running in an endless pit?

Struggling through a hopeless fit.

Troublesome and tiring, isn't it?

Find your cause, find your fight.

Pursue this battle but mind your light.

Listen to it seek where it shines most.

The battle for a cause is never ending.

When you think you've found it you've found its beginning.
